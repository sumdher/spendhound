You have 5 boxes labeled A, B, C, D, and E. Each contains a different number of coins.

Constraints:
- A has more coins than B but fewer than C.
- D has fewer coins than A.
- E has more coins than C.
- The total number of coins is 100.
- No box contains fewer than 5 coins.
- All values are integers.
- The difference between the largest and smallest box is exactly 40.
- B + D = 30

Question:
What are the exact number of coins in each box?

Explain your reasoning step by step, and verify all constraints at the end.
